About the Practice

Ivan Guyvan’s multidisciplinary work translates fleeting emotions into luminous compositions. Each series is an experiment in texture and rhythm, inviting viewers to slow down, notice the gradients between presence and memory, and reimagine their own narratives through color, form, and light.

Artist Bio

Roots

Born and raised along the Baltic coast, Ivan’s early years were shaped by fog-soaked horizons and the kinetic pulse of street art. The result: a fascination with blending organic depth and digital precision.

Process

Ivan combines analog collage, generative techniques, and layered photography. Each piece moves through sketchbooks, digital renders, and atmospheric printing to arrive at a resonant final form.

Highlights

Recent exhibitions include “Chromatic Echoes” (Tallinn), “Shifting Tides” (Berlin), and a residency with the New North Collective. Commissioned works explore identity, memory, and the tension between structure and fluidity.
